Mission

Applying the accumulated experience and knowledge in advanced technologies, we will make distant worlds closer for everyone...

Our company

The company "Space Tour" originates from the beginning of the 21st century. The company's initial goal was to explore nearby planets. But with the development of space tourism, our technology has made it possible for everyone to experience unforgettable travel experiences. Currently, the company is the leader in terms of the number of flights. Far in the past are those times when the most remote distances were limited only to our Earth. Now you can diversify your vacation by visiting the orbits of the planets of the solar system and enjoy incredible views.

Directions

Earth

You can enjoy breathtaking views of our planet on a three days-long orbital trip

Moon

A week trip to the Moon with the opportunity to set foot on its surface will not leave anyone indifferent

Mars

Mars has always attracted many explorers. Few people have not wondered if there is life on Mars. You can answer this question for yourself at the end of a three-month trip to this planet

Venus

If there is a real hell, then it is on the planet Venus and you can see it on our two-month trip

Jupiter

You can even imagine how beautiful this huge gas giant is. Once having visited its orbit, you will never forget the bizarre outlines of its clouds. The duration of the trip is not for the weak, six months

Saturn

One of the unique wonders of the solar system are the rings of Saturn. You can see with your own eyes at a very close distance if you go on a trip to Saturn. The duration of the trip is up to 7 months

Future plans

We are constantly improving our technologies to make your travels safe and comfortable. The development of new engines will reduce the time spent on the way to distant objects. Using the new engines, we plan to explore more distant objects in our solar system from Saturn to Pluto. In the future, after carrying out all the necessary research and testing, you will also have the opportunity to see these distant objects with your own eyes.
